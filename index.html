✈️ OnlyPlanes Has Been Shut Down

This project has been permanently discontinued and is no longer available.

Why was OnlyPlanes shut down?
OnlyPlanes is shutting down because it simply wasn't worth continuing.

After spending a significant amount of time building, maintaining, and hosting the platform, it became clear that the project wasn't providing enough value to justify the ongoing investment.

Running an online platform requires time, infrastructure, domains, maintenance, and constant attention. At some point, every project needs to justify those costs, and OnlyPlanes ultimately did not.

Rather than continue supporting something that wasn't moving forward, the decision was made to shut the service down, remove its data, and focus on projects with stronger long-term potential.

Thank you to everyone who visited, supported, or was simply curious enough to check it out.
